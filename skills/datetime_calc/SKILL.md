# Date/Time Calculator

Performs date arithmetic and day-of-week lookups.

## Operations

- **Days between**: "days between 2024-01-01 and 2024-12-31" → 365
- **Add days**: "add 30 days to 2024-01-15" → "2024-02-14"
- **Day of week**: "what day of the week is 2024-07-04" → "Thursday"
- **Subtract**: "subtract 10 days from 2024-03-15" → "2024-03-05"

## Date Format

Use YYYY-MM-DD format for best results.
